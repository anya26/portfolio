A conditional is a statement that performs different computations or actions depending on whether the statement evaluates to true or false.
An example of a conditional in Python is:

elif condition_2:
statement_block_2

A real life analogy of a conditional is the legal drinking age. If you are under 21 you cannot legally consume alcohol, but if you are older than 21, you can.
